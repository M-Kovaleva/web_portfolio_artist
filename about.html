Get ready to blast off on an unforgettable adventure through space!
Life in the Universe takes you on a thrilling journey to the farthest reaches of the cosmos - past ancient stars that are billions of years old and toward mysterious planets that just might host life. But this journey isn't just about distant worlds - it's also about you. You'll discover how you're connected to the universe in the most amazing way: the very atoms that make up your body were once part of those same stars and planets.
